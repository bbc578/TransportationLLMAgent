# Digital Twin Traffic Platform

This is a sample traffic knowledge base document for demos and interviews.

## Platform Role
A digital twin traffic platform supports operation monitoring, data fusion, visualization, simulation and governance decision support.

## Monitoring
The platform can aggregate speed, flow, occupancy, events, forecasts and signal plans.

## Visualization
Maps and dashboards help operators inspect congestion, incidents and forecast risks.

## Simulation
Signal timing or diversion suggestions should be tested in simulation before real-world use.

## Governance Support
The platform supports analysis and coordination, but it does not replace traffic authority decisions.

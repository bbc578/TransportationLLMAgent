# Traffic Operation Report Template

This is a sample traffic knowledge base document for demos and interviews.

## Report Structure
Daily reports should include scope, time range, metric summary, congested segment ranking, abnormal events, likely causes, response suggestions and risk notes.

## Metric Summary
Use average speed, flow, occupancy, congestion index and travel time index. Facts must come from traffic tools, not model memory.

## Congested Segment Ranking
Rank segments by congestion index and describe speed, flow, occupancy and region.

## Abnormal Events
List open or processing events and separate incident-driven congestion from recurring peak congestion.

## Response Suggestions
Suggestions may include information release, field verification, diversion guidance and coordination with operators.

## Risk Notes
Reports are decision-support material only and do not replace traffic authority decisions.
